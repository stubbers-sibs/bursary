Stubbers has routinely set aside funds for a bursary - used to support disadvantaged children attending the centre.

We also rely on the generosity of individual donors, however, we are unable to keep up with growing demand for assisted places, so we have decided to reach out for help to top up the fund.

Your support can change lives.

Many of these children will never been outside London let alone done water-sports, climbing or any other of our activities.

This is not just about fun - it can be a transformative experience:
fostering personal growth by building confidence, friendships & self-motivation

  • with the added bonus of a digital detox!

Doing something you never thought possible is a special and lasting experience.

How You Can Help:

All donations go in full to paying for children:

£51 one full day of activities

£240 two nights camp*

£492 four nights camp*

*with meals & activities

The fund is used to reduce the overall cost to a group, or to fund individuals who may be left out of their school visit.

Any amount will help - together we will make a difference.

Spread the Word - Share our mission with friends, family, and social media networks. The more people who know about our cause, the more children we can help!
